This is a personal project where I played with type and effects tools in the affinity designer to create an apocalyptic poster. When I created the poster after I made the shade effect. It was in the beginning stages of my learning to use Affinity Designer and I created a cool glow effect. Months later I came back to the design and envisioned an apocalyptic poster with the effect in the background.
